Feature Drinks

We served the best of the best drinks on this resort. The coconuts grown here and harvested with the most utter care from the owners themselves. Therefore, we recommend you trying our famous resort special, the C.Colada.

We serve fresh caught seafood to our customers on a daily basis. Featuring our most ordered dish, the C-Food Platter. It contains all the seafood you can imagine! From lobsters to crabs to fish. Everything here is caught by our staff and the owners themselves.

Scenery

The scenery here is also magnificent. The morning breeze to the sunny noon and lastly the sunset. This is the place for romantic trips and memory making! You won't forget about the scenery!
